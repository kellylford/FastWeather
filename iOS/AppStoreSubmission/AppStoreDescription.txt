Fast Weather - Clean, Accessible Weather Forecasts

Get the weather information you need, fast. No clutter, no tracking, no subscriptions—just accurate forecasts delivered in a beautifully simple interface designed for everyone.

WHY FAST WEATHER?

• Lightning Fast: Current conditions at a glance, detailed forecasts when you need them
• Built from the ground up with VoiceOver users in mind
• Your Privacy Matters: No tracking, no analytics, no ads. Your city list stays on your device
• No API Key Required: Powered by Open-Meteo's free weather service
• Two View Modes: Choose how you view your weather—Flat or List view

COMPREHENSIVE WEATHER DATA

Current Conditions:
• Temperature and "feels like" with humidity
• Wind speed, direction, and gusts
• UV Index with color-coded risk levels
• Dew point, pressure, visibility, cloud cover
• Precipitation tracking (rain, showers, snow)

Hourly Forecast:
• 24-hour outlook with detailed conditions
• Rain probability and precipitation amounts
• UV Index, wind, humidity, and more
• Perfect for planning your day

Daily Forecast:
• 16-day extended outlook
• High and low temperatures
• Sunrise, sunset, and daylight duration
• Rain probability and UV Index

Weather Around Me:
• Regional weather in all 8 directions (N, S, E, W, NE, NW, SE, SW)
• Adjustable distance radius to see the bigger picture
• Compare conditions in surrounding areas
• Explore nearby cities and their weather

Historical Weather:
• View past weather for any date going back years
• Compare the same date across multiple years
• Browse consecutive days of historical data
• Perfect for trip planning and trend analysis

PERSONALIZE YOUR EXPERIENCE

Two View Modes:
• Flat View: Beautiful cards with full weather details
• List View: Minimalist single-line efficiency

Choose Your Units:
• Temperature: Fahrenheit or Celsius
• Wind Speed: mph or km/h
• Precipitation: inches or millimeters
• Pressure: inHg or hPa
• Visibility: miles or kilometers

Customize Fields:
• Show or hide specific weather data
• Configure what appears in city list view
• Set your preferred default view mode
• Tailor the app to your needs

SMART CITY MANAGEMENT

• Browse cities by US state or international country
• Add multiple cities with one-tap
• Swipe to delete unwanted cities
• Drag to reorder in Table and List views
